My Future

In the future, I would like to be a chemical engineer since I am fascinated with chemical processes, and it would be a good career for me to test my skills on. As a chemical engineer, I would create and refine chemical manufacturing processes using the principles of chemistry, biology, physics and math, eventually producing many common products. However, the type of chemical engineering used would likely change during my lifetime; as everything gets more and more automated, computer science will likely become a major component of most industries, so I am also focusing on building my skills in that field.
